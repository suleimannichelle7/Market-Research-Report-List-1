<p><p>Coin button batteries are categorized primarily into four types based on their chemistry. LR (Alkaline) batteries offer good energy density and are widely used in low-drain devices. SR (Silver Oxide) batteries provide stable voltage and are favored in precision instruments like watches. CR (Lithium) batteries excel in high-drain devices due to their high energy capacity and longer shelf life. Other types may include zinc-air or rechargeable options, catering to specialized applications. Each type meets specific performance needs for varied usage.</p></p>
