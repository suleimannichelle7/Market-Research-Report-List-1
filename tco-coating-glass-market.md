<p><p>The TCO (Transparent Conductive Oxide) coating glass market features three primary types: ITO (Indium Tin Oxide), FTO (Fluorine Doped Tin Oxide), and AZO (Aluminum Doped Zinc Oxide). ITO is widely used in electronic displays and solar cells due to its excellent conductivity and transparency. FTO serves similar functions, particularly in solar applications, offering good thermal stability. AZO, known for its cost-effectiveness and environmental benefits, is emerging as a viable alternative, especially in optoelectronic devices and energy applications.</p></p>
