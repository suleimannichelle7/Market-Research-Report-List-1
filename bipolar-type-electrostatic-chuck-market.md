<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bipolar type electrostatic chuck market features two primary types: Coulomb and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) chucks. Coulomb-type chucks utilize electrostatic forces generated by charged plates to hold wafers securely, offering high precision and reliability. In contrast, JR-type chucks exploit dielectric materials to create electrostatic attraction, enhancing grip and reducing potential damage to sensitive substrates. Both technologies cater to semiconductor manufacturing, with their respective advantages in performance, efficiency, and suitability for specific applications, driving innovation in the industry.</p></p>
