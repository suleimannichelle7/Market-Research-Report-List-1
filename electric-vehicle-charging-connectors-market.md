<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging Connectors market is categorized into Type 1, Type 2, and Type 3 connectors. Type 1, common in North America and Asia, is a single-phase plug with a 5-pin design, suitable for slower charging. Type 2, widely used in Europe, supports both single and three-phase charging with a 7-pin design, offering faster charging speeds. Type 3, less common, is a three-phase connector with safety shutters, primarily used in older European models, but largely phased out by Type 2.</p></p>
