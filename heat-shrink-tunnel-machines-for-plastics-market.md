<p><strong>The Heat Shrink Tunnel Machines for Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convective (hot-air) Type</li><li>Radiant (Infrared) Type</li><li>Steam Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat shrink tunnel machines for plastics utilize various heating methods to shrink plastic films around products. Convective (hot-air) type machines circulate hot air to evenly heat and shrink the film, ensuring a tight fit. Radiant (infrared) type machines use infrared radiation to directly heat the surface of the film, offering quick and efficient shrinking. Steam type machines employ steam to soften the plastic, allowing it to conform closely to the product, providing a secure and polished finish. Each type serves specific applications and product requirements.</p></p>
