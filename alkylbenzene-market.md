<p><strong>The Alkylbenzene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Alkylbenzene</li><li>Branched Alkylbenzene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alkylbenzene market is primarily divided into two types: linear alkylbenzene (LAB) and branched alkylbenzene (BAB). Linear alkylbenzene, produced through straight-chain alkyl groups, is commonly used in the manufacture of detergents and surfactants due to its superior biodegradability and performance. In contrast, branched alkylbenzene features branched alkyl chains, which provide different properties, making them suitable for specialized applications, including lubricants and solvents. The choice between LAB and BAB depends on the intended application and desired performance characteristics.</p></p>
