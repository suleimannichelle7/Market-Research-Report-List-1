<p><p>Nanoparticle drug delivery systems encompass various types that enhance therapeutic efficacy and targeting. Bioadhesive microsphere drug delivery systems utilize adhesive materials to prolong drug residence time at target sites, improving absorption. Magnetic microsphere systems enable targeted drug release through controlled external magnetic fields, increasing treatment precision. Polymeric microsphere systems use biodegradable polymers to encapsulate drugs, allowing for sustained release profiles. Other variants may include lipid-based or inorganic nanoparticles, each designed to optimize delivery, enhance bioavailability, and minimize side effects.</p></p>
