<p><strong>The OSB Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OSB/1</li><li>OSB/2</li><li>OSB/3</li><li>OSB/4</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OSB (Oriented Strand Board) panels are categorized into four types based on their strength and moisture resistance. **OSB/1** is for general-purpose use in dry conditions, like furniture. **OSB/2** is stronger, suitable for load-bearing applications in dry environments, such as flooring or roofing. **OSB/3** offers higher moisture resistance, making it ideal for structural use in humid conditions, like exterior sheathing. **OSB/4** is the most durable, designed for heavy-duty structural applications with high moisture exposure, such as permanent formwork or industrial uses. Each type serves specific construction needs.</p></p>
