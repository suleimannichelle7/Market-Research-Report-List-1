<p><ul><li>Parallel Beam</li><li>Concentric Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Light Beams market comprises two primary types: Parallel Beam and Concentric Beam. Parallel Beams deliver uniform light intensity along a straight path, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent illumination, such as in stage lighting or architectural designs. In contrast, Concentric Beams feature light that radiates outward from a central point, creating a diffused effect useful for ambiance in spaces like galleries or theaters. Both beam types cater to specific lighting needs across various industries, enhancing visual experiences.</p></p>
