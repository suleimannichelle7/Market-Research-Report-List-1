<p><strong>The Oil Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensing Oil-Fired Furnace</li><li>Oil Fired Indirect Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil furnace market is primarily categorized into two types: condensing oil-fired furnaces and oil-fired indirect heaters. Condensing oil-fired furnaces are designed to achieve higher efficiency by capturing and reusing exhaust gases, resulting in lower fuel consumption and emissions. On the other hand, oil-fired indirect heaters use oil to heat water or air indirectly, making them suitable for applications requiring consistent heat delivery, such as residential and industrial heating systems. Both types cater to varying consumer needs for energy efficiency and performance.</p></p>
