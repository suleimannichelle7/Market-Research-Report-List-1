<p><p>The photonic integrated devices market encompasses various components essential for optical communication and signal processing. Lasers generate coherent light for transmission, while modulators control the intensity or phase of the light signal. Detectors convert optical signals into electrical signals, ensuring data is received accurately. Attenuators manage signal strength, preventing distortion. Multiplexers/De-Multiplexers combine or separate multiple signals for efficient transmission. Optical amplifiers boost signal strength, enhancing distance and reliability in fiber optic networks, driving advancements in telecommunications and data centers.</p></p>
