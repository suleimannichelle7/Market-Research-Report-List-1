<p><ul><li>Forged</li><li>Cast rods</li><li>Powder Metals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive connecting rod market comprises three main types: forged, cast, and powder metal rods. Forged rods are made by shaping heated metal, offering superior strength and durability, making them ideal for high-performance engines. Cast rods are created by pouring molten metal into molds, allowing for complex shapes at a lower cost, though they may be less robust. Powder metal rods are manufactured from metal powders that are compressed and sintered, providing a unique combination of strength and lightweight characteristics, often used in specialized applications.</p></p>
