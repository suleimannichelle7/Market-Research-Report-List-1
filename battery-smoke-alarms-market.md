<p><p>The battery smoke alarm market is primarily divided into three types: photoelectric, ionization, and others. Photoelectric type alarms utilize a light beam to detect smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization type alarms, on the other hand, rely on radioactive materials to sense fast-flaming fires. The 'others' category may include combination alarms, smart alarms, or specialized models designed for specific environments. Each type offers distinct advantages catering to various safety needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
