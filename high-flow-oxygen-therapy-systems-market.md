<p><p>High Flow Oxygen Therapy Systems encompass two main types: Automatic Oxygen Adjustment and Manual Oxygen Adjustment. Automatic systems dynamically adjust oxygen levels based on real-time patient data, ensuring optimal delivery without manual intervention, enhancing comfort and efficacy. Conversely, Manual Oxygen Adjustment systems require healthcare providers to manually set and monitor oxygen flow rates, offering more control in specific scenarios but necessitating closer oversight. Both types are integral to managing respiratory conditions, improving patient outcomes through tailored oxygen therapy.</p></p>
