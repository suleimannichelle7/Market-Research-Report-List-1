<p><p>The tartaric acid market is categorized into three main types: L-Tartaric Acid, D-Tartaric Acid, and DL-Tartaric Acid. L-Tartaric Acid, the naturally occurring form, is primarily used in the food and beverage industry as a stabilizing agent and acidity regulator. D-Tartaric Acid, its synthetic counterpart, is employed in various chemical applications. DL-Tartaric Acid is a racemic mixture of the two forms, commonly utilized in the pharmaceutical and cosmetic industries for its properties as an acidulant and stabilizer.</p></p>
