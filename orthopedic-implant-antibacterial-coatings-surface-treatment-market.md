ASM involves altering implant surfaces to actively release antibacterial agents, preventing infections. PSM focuses on creating smooth or textured surfaces to reduce bacterial adhesion without active agents. LCC uses coatings or carriers to deliver localized antibacterial effects, enhancing implant biocompatibility and infection resistance. These methods improve implant performance and patient outcomes by addressing infection risks.</p></p>
