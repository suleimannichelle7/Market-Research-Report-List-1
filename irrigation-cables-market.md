<p><ul><li>Single Core</li><li>Multi-core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The irrigation cables market comprises two primary types: single core and multi-core cables. Single core cables consist of one conductive wire, making them suitable for straightforward irrigation applications where simplicity and cost-effectiveness are priorities. On the other hand, multi-core cables contain multiple conductors, enabling more complex installations and allowing for simultaneous operation of various irrigation systems. Multi-core cables offer improved flexibility and enhanced performance, catering to larger agricultural operations with diverse irrigation needs.</p></p>
