<p><p>Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are critical in treating cerebral aneurysms. The market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Embolic Coils** - Small metal coils inserted into the aneurysm to induce clotting and stabilize the vessel.</p><p>2. **Flow Diversion Devices** - Stents designed to redirect blood flow away from the aneurysm, promoting healing of the vessel wall.</p><p>3. **Liquid Embolic Agents** - Injected substances that solidify and block blood flow within the aneurysm directly. Each type offers unique benefits based on clinical needs.</p></p>
