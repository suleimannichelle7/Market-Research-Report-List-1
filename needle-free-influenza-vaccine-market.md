<p><p>The Needle-Free Influenza Vaccine market primarily includes two types: Live Attenuated Influenza Vaccine (LAIV) and Split Influenza Vaccine. LAIV uses weakened viruses to stimulate an immune response, offering the benefit of a nasal spray delivery method, which can enhance patient comfort and adherence. Split Influenza Vaccines, on the other hand, contain inactivated virus particles and are typically delivered via injection, which may require traditional needles. Both types aim to provide effective prevention against influenza while minimizing the discomfort associated with standard vaccination methods.</p></p>
