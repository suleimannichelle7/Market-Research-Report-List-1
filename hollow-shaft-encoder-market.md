<p><strong>The Hollow Shaft Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Hollow Shaft Encoder</li><li>Absolute Hollow Shaft Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hollow shaft encoder market is divided into two main types: incremental and absolute hollow shaft encoders. Incremental hollow shaft encoders provide position feedback by counting pulses as the shaft rotates, making them ideal for applications requiring speed and position monitoring. In contrast, absolute hollow shaft encoders offer precise position data by providing a unique code for each shaft position, ensuring reliable performance even after power loss. Together, these encoders serve diverse industries, enhancing automation and control systems.</p></p>
