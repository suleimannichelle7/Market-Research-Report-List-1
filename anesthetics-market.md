<p><p>The anesthetics market is categorized into three primary types: local anesthetics, regional anesthetics, and general anesthetics. Local anesthetics numb a specific area to prevent pain during minor procedures, while regional anesthetics block sensation in larger body regions, often used in surgeries like cesarean sections. General anesthetics induce a complete loss of consciousness and sensation, utilized for major surgeries. Each type plays a crucial role in pain management and surgical procedures, catering to varying patient needs and procedural requirements.</p></p>
