Room Temperature shakers operate at ambient conditions, suitable for general laboratory applications and routine mixing tasks. High Temperature shakers are designed for applications requiring elevated temperatures, enabling processes like cell culture or chemical reactions that benefit from increased heat. Each type caters to specific experimental needs.</p></p>
