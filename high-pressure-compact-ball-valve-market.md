<p><p>The high-pressure compact ball valve market consists primarily of single ball and double ball types. Single ball valves feature a single spherical disc that controls flow, offering simplicity and efficient sealing in high-pressure applications. In contrast, double ball valves incorporate two balls, providing enhanced flow control and redundancy, making them suitable for more complex systems requiring greater reliability. Both types are used in industries such as oil, gas, and manufacturing, where they ensure efficient operation under extreme pressure conditions.</p></p>
