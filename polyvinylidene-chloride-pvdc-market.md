<p><p>Polyvinylidene Chloride (PVDC) is a synthetic polymer known for its excellent barrier properties, making it valuable in packaging applications. In the PVDC market, types include isotactic and atactic variants. Isotactic PVDC possesses a regular, ordered structure, resulting in enhanced crystallinity and strength, ideal for durable packaging materials. Conversely, atactic PVDC features a more random structure, leading to flexibility and transparency, making it suitable for applications requiring pliability. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
