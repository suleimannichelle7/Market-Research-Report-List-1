<p><p>The automotive sunroof motor market is primarily categorized into two types: DC brushed motors and DC brushless motors. DC brushed motors utilize brushes to transfer electricity, providing a cost-effective solution for sunroof operations, but they experience more wear over time. In contrast, DC brushless motors offer higher efficiency and longevity, employing electronic controls for smoother operation and reduced maintenance. As automakers prioritize reliability and performance, the demand for brushless motors is steadily rising alongside the need for traditional brushed options.</p></p>
