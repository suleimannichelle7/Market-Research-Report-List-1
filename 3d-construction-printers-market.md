<p><strong>The 3D Construction Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Powder Bonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D construction printer market is primarily categorized into extrusion and powder bonding types. Extrusion-based printers utilize a continuous flow of material, such as concrete or polymers, to create structures layer by layer, offering flexibility in design and rapid construction. In contrast, powder bonding technology involves depositing layers of powdered materials, such as sand or metal, which are then fused together using binding agents or heat. Both methods enhance efficiency, reduce waste, and enable innovative architectural possibilities in construction.</p></p>
