<p><p>The gas masks market includes several types designed for specific uses. Disposable respirators are lightweight and intended for single use, often for short-term protection. Air Purifying Respirators (APRs) filter contaminants from the air, suitable for various environments. Powered Air Purifying Respirators (PAPRs) use a battery-powered blower for enhanced airflow and filtering. Self-contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBAs) provide breathable air from a tank, vital for hazardous situations. Additionally, the "Other" category encompasses specialized equipment not covered by these types.</p></p>
