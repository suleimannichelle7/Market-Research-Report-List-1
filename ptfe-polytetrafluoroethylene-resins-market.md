<p><p>The PTFE resins market is primarily categorized into two production methods: dispersion and suspension. The dispersion method involves creating a homogeneous mixture of PTFE particles in a liquid medium, resulting in fine powders that are used in coatings and lubricants. In contrast, the suspension method produces larger particles through the polymerization of tetrafluoroethylene in a liquid suspension, leading to applications in seals and gaskets. Both methods cater to varying industrial needs, impacting performance characteristics and end-use applications in diverse sectors.</p></p>
