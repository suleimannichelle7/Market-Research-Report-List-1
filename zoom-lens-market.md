<p><p>The Zoom Lens market encompasses three main types: Optical Zoom, Extended Optical Zoom, and Digital Zoom. Optical Zoom uses lens elements to magnify the image without loss of quality, ideal for capturing distant subjects. Extended Optical Zoom further enhances this capability by extending the zoom range while maintaining clarity. Digital Zoom, on the other hand, crops the image and enlarges it, which can result in a loss of detail. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing choices for consumers and professionals alike.</p></p>
