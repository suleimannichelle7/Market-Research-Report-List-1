<p><ul><li>Flat Stainless Steel Fiber</li><li>Hooked Stainless Steel Fiber</li><li>Undulated Stainless Steel Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel fiber market encompasses three main types: flat, hooked, and undulated fibers. Flat stainless steel fibers are characterized by their thin, flat profiles, optimizing surface contact for enhanced adhesion. Hooked stainless steel fibers feature bent ends, promoting better interlocking within concrete, improving tensile strength. Undulated stainless steel fibers have wavy patterns that increase flexibility and resistance to cracking within composite materials. Each type serves specific applications in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors, enhancing material performance and durability.</p></p>
