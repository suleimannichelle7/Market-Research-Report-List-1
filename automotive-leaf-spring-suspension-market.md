<p><p>The automotive leaf spring suspension market includes two main types: multi-leaf springs and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple layers of spring steel, offering enhanced load distribution, durability, and flexibility, making them ideal for heavier vehicles and off-road applications. In contrast, mono-leaf springs use a single, thicker leaf, providing a lightweight solution with improved handling and responsiveness, suitable for lighter vehicles. Both types are crucial in ensuring vehicle stability, comfort, and efficient load-carrying capacity.</p></p>
