<p><strong>The Piezo Ceramic Element Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Radiation Type</li><li>Indirect Radiation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezo ceramic element market comprises two main types: direct radiation and indirect radiation. Direct radiation type elements emit sound waves directly from the ceramic surface, commonly used in ultrasonic cleaning and medical imaging applications. In contrast, indirect radiation type elements utilize a medium, such as air or water, to transmit sound waves generated by the ceramic, making them suitable for applications like sonar and acoustic sensing. Both types leverage piezoelectric properties for diverse industrial and medical uses.</p></p>
