<p><p>An Autonomous Emergency Braking System (AEBS) is an advanced safety feature in vehicles designed to automatically apply brakes when a potential collision is detected. AEBS is increasingly being adopted in passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and public transportation systems.</p><p>The Autonomous Emergency Braking System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period. The integration of AI and machine learning in AEBS technology is further enhancing its accuracy and reliability, making it a critical component in modern vehicles.</p><p>Emerging trends in the AEBS market include the development of low-speed AEBS for urban driving, the incorporation of pedestrian and cyclist detection capabilities, and the use of connected vehicle technology for real-time data sharing. Additionally, the expansion of electric vehicle (EV) production is creating new opportunities for AEBS adoption, as EVs often prioritize advanced safety features.
