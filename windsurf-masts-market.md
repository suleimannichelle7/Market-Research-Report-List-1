<p><strong>The Windsurf Masts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SDM Masts</li><li>RDM Masts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The windsurf mast market is primarily divided into two types: SDM (Standard Diameter Masts) and RDM (Reduced Diameter Masts). SDM masts are thicker and offer greater stiffness, providing stability and performance in high-wind conditions, making them popular among advanced riders. In contrast, RDM masts have a smaller diameter, which enhances flexibility, control, and durability, making them suitable for a wider range of skill levels and conditions. Both types cater to distinct preferences in windsurfing, impacting overall performance and experience.</p></p>
