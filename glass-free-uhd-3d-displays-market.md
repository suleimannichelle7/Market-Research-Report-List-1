<p><p>Glass-free UHD 3D displays utilize advanced technologies like parallax barrier and lenticular display to create immersive three-dimensional visuals without the need for glasses. Parallax barrier technology involves a slatted barrier that directs light to each eye, enabling depth perception. In contrast, lenticular display technology uses a series of lenses to manipulate light direction, presenting different images to each eye for a more dynamic viewing experience. Both approaches cater to various applications, enhancing entertainment, gaming, and advertising experiences in the market.</p></p>
