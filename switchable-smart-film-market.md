<p><p>The Switchable Smart Film market is primarily categorized into two types: Self-Adhesive Smart Film and Smart Film for Lamination. Self-Adhesive Smart Film is designed for easy installation on existing glass surfaces, allowing for quick transformation between transparent and opaque states without the need for additional laminating processes. In contrast, Smart Film for Lamination is integrated during the manufacturing of glass, providing a seamless, durable finish ideal for architectural applications. Both types enhance privacy and control over light transmission in various settings.</p></p>
