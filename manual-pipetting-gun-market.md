<p><p>The manual pipetting gun market is mainly divided into two types: single-channel and multichannel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes are designed for dispensing liquids one channel at a time, making them ideal for precise measurements in applications requiring accuracy. In contrast, multichannel pipettes allow for simultaneous dispensing across multiple channels, enhancing efficiency in tasks like high-throughput screening and repetitive sample processing. Both types cater to diverse laboratory needs, improving productivity and precision in liquid handling procedures.</p></p>
