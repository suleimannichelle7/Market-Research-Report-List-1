<p><p>The Forward Collision Avoidance Radar market comprises three main types: Ultrasonic Radar, Laser Radar, and Microwave Radar. Ultrasonic Radar uses sound waves to detect obstacles, suitable for short-range applications. Laser Radar employs light waves for precise distance measurements, ideal for high-speed detection and environmental mapping. Microwave Radar utilizes high-frequency radio waves, offering effective obstacle detection over longer ranges and in varying weather conditions. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety by preventing collisions.</p></p>
