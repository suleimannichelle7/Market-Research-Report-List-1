<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Color Sorters</li><li>Belt-Type Color Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color sorter market is primarily categorized into two types: chute-type and belt-type color sorters. Chute-type color sorters utilize a vertical drop mechanism, allowing materials to flow through a chute where they are analyzed for color and quality, making them ideal for bulk items like grains and seeds. Belt-type color sorters, on the other hand, transport products on a conveyor belt, offering precise sorting and higher efficiency for irregularly shaped items like fruits and vegetables. Both types enhance product quality and reduce waste.</p></p>
