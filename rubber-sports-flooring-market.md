<p><p>The Rubber Sports Flooring market is categorized into two main types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber, derived from latex, offers excellent elasticity and shock absorption, making it ideal for various sports applications. It is eco-friendly but may require more maintenance. Synthetic rubber, on the other hand, is engineered from petrochemical sources, providing durability and resistance to wear and moisture. This type often features enhanced customization options and lower maintenance needs, making it popular in diverse sports environments.</p></p>
