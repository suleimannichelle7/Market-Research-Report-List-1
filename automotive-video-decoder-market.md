<p><p>The automotive video decoder market is segmented into dual-channel and four-channel types, catering to varying vehicle video processing needs. Dual-channel decoders support two video inputs simultaneously, ideal for basic functions like rearview and front-facing cameras. In contrast, four-channel decoders manage up to four videos at once, enabling advanced features such as 360-degree vision systems and enhanced driver assistance technologies. The choice between these types depends on vehicle complexity and the desired level of safety and technological integration.</p></p>
