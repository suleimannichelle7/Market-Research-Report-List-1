<p><ul><li>AN(Naturally-Cooled) Type</li><li>AF(Forced-air-Cooled) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cast resin dry-type transformers are classified into two main types based on their cooling methods: AN (Naturally-Cooled) and AF (Forced-Air-Cooled). AN type transformers utilize natural convection for cooling, making them suitable for environments where low maintenance and durability are essential. Conversely, AF type transformers incorporate forced air circulation to enhance cooling efficiency, enabling higher power ratings and compact designs. Both types serve in various applications, including industrial, commercial, and renewable energy sectors, offering improved safety and environmental performance.</p></p>
