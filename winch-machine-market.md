Worm gear winches offer a mechanical advantage and self-locking capabilities, suitable for controlled lowering of loads. Capstan winches are versatile for pulling or tensioning ropes and cables, commonly used in marine and industrial settings. Other winch types include electric and hydraulic variations, serving diverse operational needs across different industries.</p></p>
