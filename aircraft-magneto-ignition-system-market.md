<p><strong>The Aircraft Magneto Ignition System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Tension Ignition Systems</li><li>Low-Tension Ignition Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft magneto ignition system market is primarily divided into high-tension and low-tension ignition systems. High-tension ignition systems utilize a magneto to generate high-voltage electricity, sending it directly to the spark plugs, ensuring reliable ignition even under varying conditions. In contrast, low-tension ignition systems employ lower voltage, using a series of components to facilitate spark generation. These systems are generally lighter and simpler but may provide less reliability compared to high-tension systems, influencing their application in different aircraft types.</p></p>
