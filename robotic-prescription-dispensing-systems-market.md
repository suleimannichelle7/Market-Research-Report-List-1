<p><ul><li>Centralized Dispensing Systems</li><li>Decentralized Dispensing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Robotic Prescription Dispensing Systems are categorized into two main types: centralized and decentralized dispensing systems. Centralized dispensing systems operate from a single, centralized location, allowing for efficient bulk processing and inventory management, typically found in large hospitals or pharmacy hubs. In contrast, decentralized dispensing systems are situated closer to patient care areas, enabling quicker access to medications and enhancing workflow efficiency in smaller healthcare settings. Both types aim to improve accuracy, reduce human error, and optimize pharmacy operations.</p></p>
