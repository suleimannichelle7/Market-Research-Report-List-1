<p><p>The Image Sensor Test System market comprises three main types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor), CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), and CIS (Contact Image Sensor). CMOS sensors are widely used in consumer electronics due to their low power consumption and high-speed processing. CCD sensors excel in high-quality imaging and low noise, making them suitable for professional applications. CIS technology is primarily used in scanning devices, offering efficient image capture with a compact design. Each type serves various industries, including automotive, medical, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
