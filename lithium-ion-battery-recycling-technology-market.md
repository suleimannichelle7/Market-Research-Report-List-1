<p><p>The lithium-ion battery recycling technology market encompasses two main types: cascade utilization and traditional recycling. Cascade utilization involves repurposing used batteries for less demanding applications, extending their lifespan and reducing waste. In contrast, traditional recycling focuses on recovering valuable materials, such as lithium, cobalt, and nickel, through processes that safely dismantle and refine batteries. Both approaches aim to enhance sustainability, minimize environmental impact, and promote the circular economy by recovering resources and reducing the need for raw material extraction.</p></p>
