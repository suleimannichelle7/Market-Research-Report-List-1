<p><p>The marine lighting market comprises four primary types: LED, fluorescent, halogen, and xenon. LED lighting is energy-efficient, long-lasting, and resistant to harsh marine environments, making it increasingly popular. Fluorescent lights offer good illumination at a lower cost but may not withstand moisture as well. Halogen lights provide bright, focused light and are often used for spot illumination, while xenon lights deliver a high-intensity output, ideal for performance-focused applications, though they consume more power than LEDs.</p></p>
