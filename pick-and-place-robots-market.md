<p><strong>The Pick and Place Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Articulated</li><li>Delta</li><li>SCARA</li><li>Cartesian</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pick and place robots are categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Articulated**: These robots have rotary joints, offering flexibility for various tasks in complex environments. </p><p>2. **Delta**: Characterized by their parallel arms, they excel in high-speed applications, ideal for packaging and assembly. </p><p>3. **SCARA**: With a horizontal arm and vertical movement, SCARA robots are efficient for tasks requiring precise horizontal placements. </p><p>4. **Cartesian**: Utilizing linear movements on X, Y, and Z axes, Cartesian robots are easy to program and suited for straightforward pick and place tasks.</p></p>
