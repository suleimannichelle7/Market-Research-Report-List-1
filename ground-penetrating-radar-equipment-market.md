<p><p>The Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: handheld and cart-based systems. Handheld GPR equipment is compact, portable, and designed for quick, localized scans, making it ideal for smaller areas, utility inspections, and archaeological studies. In contrast, cart-based GPR systems are larger, mounted on wheeled platforms, and provide comprehensive data collection over extensive areas, suitable for construction, geology, and environmental assessments. Both types use radar pulses to detect subsurface structures and variations.</p></p>
