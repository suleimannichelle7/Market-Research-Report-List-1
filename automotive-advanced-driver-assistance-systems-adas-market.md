<p><p>Automotive Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) are technologies designed to enhance vehicle safety and driving efficiency by assisting drivers in various tasks. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 4.9% during the forecast period.
