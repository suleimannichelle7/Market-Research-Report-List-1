<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Handheld Laser Telemeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase Method Telemeter</li><li>Pulse Method Telemeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld laser telemeter market is primarily divided into two types: phase method telemeters and pulse method telemeters. Phase method telemeters calculate distance by measuring the phase shift of a laser beam between the target and the device. In contrast, pulse method telemeters determine distance by timing how long it takes for a pulse of laser light to travel to the target and back. Both types offer high precision for various applications, including construction, surveying, and industrial measurement.</p></p>
