<p><strong>The Internal Urinary Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indwelling Catheters</li><li>Intermittent Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The internal urinary catheters market primarily consists of two main types: indwelling catheters and intermittent catheters. Indwelling catheters, or Foley catheters, are designed for prolonged use, continuously draining urine from the bladder, making them suitable for patients with long-term urinary retention needs. Intermittent catheters, on the other hand, are used on a temporary basis, allowing patients to self-catheterize at regular intervals. This type promotes bladder function and minimizes infection risks, catering to those needing short-term assistance.</p></p>
