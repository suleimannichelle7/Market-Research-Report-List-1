<p><strong>The Companion Animal Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Attenuated Live Vaccines</li><li>Conjugate Vaccines</li><li>Inactivated Vaccines</li><li>Subunit Vaccines</li><li>Toxoid Vaccines</li><li>DNA Vaccines</li><li>Recombinant Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The companion animal vaccines market is diverse, comprising various types. Attenuated live vaccines use weakened pathogens to elicit strong immune responses. Inactivated vaccines contain killed pathogens, providing safety but often needing boosters. Subunit vaccines incorporate specific antigen pieces for targeted immunity, while conjugate vaccines enhance responses by linking polysaccharides to proteins. Toxoid vaccines neutralize toxins from pathogens. DNA vaccines introduce genetic material to stimulate responses, and recombinant vaccines use genetically engineered organisms to produce antigens, offering advanced protection strategies for pets.</p></p>
