<p><ul><li>Salient Pole Type</li><li>Smooth Cylindrical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Alternator Market features two primary types: Salient Pole Type and Smooth Cylindrical Type. Salient Pole Type alternators have protruding poles, offering high torque at lower speeds, making them efficient for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, Smooth Cylindrical Type alternators have a cylindrical rotor design that allows for higher speed operation, ideal for applications requiring consistent power generation. Each type serves specific needs in commercial vehicles, balancing performance, efficiency, and energy reliability in diverse driving conditions.</p></p>
