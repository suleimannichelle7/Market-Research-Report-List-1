<p><p>The Catalytic Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) market comprises primarily two types: Silicon Carbide (SiC) DPF and Cordierite DPF. SiC DPFs are known for their high thermal durability and efficiency in capturing soot, making them suitable for demanding environments and heavy-duty applications. Cordierite DPFs, on the other hand, offer lower thermal conductivity and are lighter, making them ideal for light-duty vehicles. Both types play a crucial role in reducing emissions and complying with environmental regulations in the automotive industry.</p></p>
