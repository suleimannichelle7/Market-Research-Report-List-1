<p><strong>The Veterinary Capnography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mainstream Capnographys</li><li>Sidestream Capnographys</li><li>Microstream Capnographys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary capnography market encompasses three main types: mainstream capnographs, sidestream capnographs, and microstream capnographs. Mainstream capnographs measure CO2 directly at the airway, providing real-time data for immediate analysis. Sidestream capnographs sample gas from a distance, allowing for versatility in monitoring without obstructing the airway. Microstream capnographs use a low flow sample, reducing the workload on the animal while providing accurate readings. Each type serves distinct needs within veterinary practices, enhancing patient monitoring and care.</p></p>
