<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring</li><li>Homogeneous Sheet Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sheet flooring market is primarily divided into two types: heterogeneous and homogeneous sheet flooring. Heterogeneous sheet flooring consists of multiple layers, featuring a design layer, a backing layer, and a durable wear layer, offering a variety of aesthetics and added cushioning. In contrast, homogeneous sheet flooring is made from a single material composition, ensuring a consistent color and pattern throughout, which enhances durability and ease of maintenance. Both types cater to different aesthetic and functional needs in various environments.</p></p>
